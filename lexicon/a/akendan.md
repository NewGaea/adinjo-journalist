## Etymology
From [***a-***](lexicon/a/a-) "great, grand" and [***kendan***](lexicon/k/kendan) "legend, myth, fantasy story"

## Pronunciation
- **IPA Broad**: /f.netiks/

## Noun

### akendan (plural *akendanu*)
1. epic, high tale, mythical story
2. grand fantasy, great legend
