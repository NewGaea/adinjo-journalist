akendan
=======

**akendan** _noun_

`a ken dan`

1. legend
2. myth
3. fantasy

`#noun` `#universal`

### Other Forms

### Derivation

**Akendan** is a straightforward derivation of the prefix **a-** _great_ and the root **kendan** _story, tale_.

### Related Words

> **a** _prefix_ great

> **kendan** _noun_ story, tale
