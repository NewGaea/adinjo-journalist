# andèn

# Adinjo
## Alternative Forms
- **anden** (implied stress)
## Etymology
It is uncertain if there is a verb underlying **andèn**, made stative by the [**an-**](lexicon/a/an) prefix, or if **andèn** is a root of its own.
## Pronunciation
- **IPA Broad**: /an.d3n/

## Descriptor

### andèn
1. (source) divine
2. (personal) holy
3. (quality) holy, sacred
