# anon

# Adinjo
## Alternative Forms

## Etymology
Indefinite. From [**an- (1)**](lexicon/a/an), "sex, gender" and an unknown element. There is a suggestion that it derives from [**kon**](lexicon/k/kon) "metal".

## Pronunciation
- **IPA Broad**: /an.Ëˆon/

## Descriptor

### anon
1. genderless, ungendered
2. (grammar) neuter
3. (identity) agender
