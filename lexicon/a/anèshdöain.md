# Adinjo
## Alternative Forms

## Etymology
From [**anèsh**](lexicon/a/anèsh) "lightning, electricity" and [**döain**](lexicon/d/döain) "river", refers 

## Pronunciation
- **IPA Broad**: /f.netiks/

## Noun

### anèshdöain (plural *anèshdöainu*)
1. lightning bolt, electrical arc
2. ley line
3. magical discharge
