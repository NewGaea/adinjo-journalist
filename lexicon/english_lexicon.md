## Conventions

- This lexicon lists all verbs with the based on their Adinjo form:
  - Verbs ending with _-ing_ or equivalent in English are **ton verbs,** which are only listed in their gerund form
    - The full stop mark _._ separates the infinitive root from the gerund ending of **ton verbs**, and should not be written when composing Adinjo.
  - Verbs ending without the _-ing_ ending are **kun verbs,** some of which are also listed in their inflected forms
- Any dash _-_ in an Adinjo word indicates an affix point, or the direction(s) this entry connects to other words.
