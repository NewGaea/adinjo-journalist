## Conventions

- This lexicon lists all English verbs with the _-ing_ ending or equivalent.
- The full stop mark _._ separates the infinitive root from the gerund ending of **ton verbs**, and should not be written when composing Adinjo.
- Any dash _-_ in an Adinjo word indicates an affix point, or the direction(s) this entry connects to other words.
